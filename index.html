    Ask Your Questions

    Regular AC servicing once or twice a year ensures optimal performance and prevents summer breakdowns.

    Inadequate cooling may be due to clogged filters, low refrigerant, dirty coils, or a faulty thermostat. A professional service can quickly diagnose and fix the issue.

    Unusual noises may be due to a faulty fan, compressor issues, or ice buildup. If the noise persists, contact a technician for inspection.

    If your refrigerator isn’t freezing properly, check the temperature setting, clean the condenser coils, ensure the door seal is intact, and remove any frost buildup. If the issue persists, seek professional servicing.
